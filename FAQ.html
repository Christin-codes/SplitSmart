Account setup and Management

How do I create an account on the app? +

Download the Splitsmart app from Google playstore, open the app and click on the "sign up" button

What documents are required for verification (KYC)? +

How do I reset my password or pin? +

Click on the "forgotten password" button in your screen and follow the laid out steps to retrieve your password or change it.

Go to your profile information and click on the settings to find the "Log out" button or "deactivation" button.
