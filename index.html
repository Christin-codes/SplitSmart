About Us

What is SplitSmart?
SplitSmart helps friends, families, and teams manage group expenses without drama. From splitting bills to tracking dues, we make payments clear, secure, and stress-free.

Why We Built It
We created SplitSmart to solve the everyday problem of managing group money. Whether it's rent with roommates, church contributions, or hangouts with friends — we wanted a tool that simplifies payment, reduces stress, and keeps everyone accountable.

Who It's For
SplitSmart is built for:

Students sharing rent or food money

Church or social groups collecting dues

Families managing home expenses

Friends going on trips or organizing events

If you share or collect money with others — SplitSmart Is For You.
