SPLITSMART PRIVACY POLICY

Welcome to SplitSmart's Privacy Policy.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our website or mobile services.

What Data We Collect

When you use SplitSmart, we may collect the following information:

Personal Identifiable Information (PII)

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Bank account/payment information (via third-party processors)
  • Profile Picture (optional)

Usage Data

  • App usage logs (pages visited, actions performed)
  • Device and browser type
  • IP address and location (approximate)
  • Transaction details (amounts, group names, payees)

Third-party Integrations

  • Payment gateway tokens (e.g., Flutterwave, Paystack)
  • Contacts, with your permission (to invite or split with others)

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage user accounts and groups
  • Track and split expenses between group members
  • Send reminders and payment links
  • Process transactions via third-party providers
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Ensure compliance with laws and prevent fraud

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data:

  • With your consent (e.g., when signing up or enabling contacts)
  • To perform a contract (e.g., expense tracking and payment settlement)
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., KYC/AML laws)
  • For legitimate interests (e.g., app improvement, analytics, fraud prevention)

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share data with:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Paystack, Flutterwave) to facilitate transactions
  • Regulators or law enforcement if required by law
  • Cloud hosting providers (e.g., AWS or local servers)
  • Internal team members and support staff, under strict confidentiality

We ensure all third parties comply with NDPR and global data protection standards.

Data Security

We take your data seriously. Measures include:

  • End-to-end encryption (SSL/TLS)
  • Encrypted payment tokens
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Routine vulnerability scans and security patches
  • Access controls and role-based data access internally

While we do our best to keep your data safe, we recommend using extra measures like two-factor authentication for added protection.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct or update inaccuracies
  • Delete your account and data
  • Withdraw consent to certain data uses
  • Object to data usage or ask us to restrict data
  • Port your data for use in another app
  • File complaints with the Nigerian Data Protection Commission

Data Retention

We store your data:

  • While your account is active
  • As legally needed (e.g., for reporting or audits)
  • In anonymized form to understand app usage trends
  • After extended inactivity ([insert timeframe], e.g., 12 months) we may delete or anonymize it

Cookies & Similar Tracking

We use cookies and similar tools to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Analyze app usage and performance
  • Show relevant ads to free users

You can manage cookies through your browser; disabling some may limit our app's functionality.

Children's Privacy

SplitSmart is not intended for users under the age of 18. If we discover a user is under this age, we will delete their data unless parental consent is obtained.

Cross-Border Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers outside Nigeria. We ensure adequate protection is in place in compliance with the NDPR and international standards for such transfers.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Users will be notified via:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email or public update on our website

Please check this page regularly for updates.
